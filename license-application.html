Requirements

What each province asks for.

Ontario

Basic training (40 hrs), a passing exam score, a clear criminal record check, and a $80 provincial filing fee. Licenses are typically issued within 2–3 weeks.

Alberta

Basic training, a passing exam score, and a $60 provincial filing fee. Processing generally runs 3–4 weeks.

British Columbia

Basic training, a passing exam score, and a $75 provincial filing fee. This is typically the fastest turnaround, around 2 weeks.

Manitoba

Basic training, a passing exam score, and a $70 provincial filing fee. Processing generally runs about 3 weeks.
