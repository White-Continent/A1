Questions

Frequently asked

How long does licensing take?

Most applicants finish training, exam and application within 3–6 weeks, depending on provincial exam availability.

Do requirements differ by province?

Yes. Training hours, fees and exam formats are set by each province. Your checklist adjusts automatically once you select yours.

I already completed training elsewhere — can I skip that step?

Yes — choose the Application only plan and upload your existing completion certificate for review.

What if my application gets rejected?

Support reviews rejection notices with you and helps refile at no extra cost for document-related issues.
